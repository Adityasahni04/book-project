About The Author

Get to know the mind

Himanshu Sharma

Author & Teacher

Himanshu Sharma is an author, speaker, and teacher who helps individuals find clarity and purpose. After experiencing burnout, he simplified life, reduced stress, and embraced mindfulness. He now works with individuals and organizations, including NGOs, to guide people through challenges and achieve balance.

In addition to his speaking engagements Himanshu shares insights on living authentically and with intention in an increasingly complicated world through his writing. His work aims to help people navigate life's complexities with a sense of purpose and peace.
